Idea and Features

RND|Roll is a TV Guide Online Magazine for Tabletop Roll Play (TTRPG) / Pen-&-Paper (PnP) games.

As it is possible to record/live stream Dungeon & Dragon (DnD), Warhammer, Pathfinder, HeroQuest, How-to-be-a-Hero, and many other games and variants, it is challenging to keep track of them.
Most of the time, one stumbles on a Twitch or YouTube Series by accident.

Here one can make a quick post of any event they want to make or promote.
One can also create characters to add to an event.
This way, similar to a TV Guide, it is possible to gather some rudimental details on the game and setting.
So it is easier to find events of interest.

Events can be edited, postponed, or even handed over to someone else if needed.
Changing the owner of an event will require a site admin and should only be done in exceptions.

To interact with the options on this website, registration is necessary.
Otherwise, this website is just read-only information on upcoming events.

Code of Conduct

Honest and respectful conduct is expected.
Failure to meet this or any other action due to the lack of mental maturity will be appropriately handled and result in the removal of the user.

Please keep any comments on the topic of the related event/show.
Constructive feedback and comments are great and the way to go.

Any quotes must be marked as a quotation, including from whom and when/ where.

Constructive time-management is encouraged or, in other words:
Don't waste your time and energy on squabbling, trolling, being pious, edgy, or insulting.

There is an exception:
Roleplay
As a website that showcases roleplaying games, a User playing a character in an event is allowed to comment 'in character' with all the swearing and quirks said character has.
The same goes for any NPC played by the Game Master.
These comments 'in character' must be marked as such roleplay.
